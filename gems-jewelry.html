India's Jewelry Heritage

India has been renowned for its jewelry craftsmanship for millennia, creating some of the world's most exquisite pieces. From traditional Kundan and Meenakari work to contemporary diamond jewelry, our network connects you with master jewelers and gem traders who combine ancient techniques with modern precision.

India is one of the world's largest centers for jewelry manufacturing and gem processing. Our extensive network includes certified jewelers, diamond manufacturers, and gem dealers who maintain the highest standards of quality and authenticity while offering competitive pricing.

Specialty Styles

We specialize in sourcing from India's renowned jewelry hubs, each known for specific styles and techniques:

  • Rajasthani Kundan and Meenakari work
  • South Indian temple jewelry
  • Jadau and traditional setting techniques
  • Contemporary diamond jewelry
  • Handcrafted silver jewelry
  • Tribal and folk jewelry designs

Quality & Certification

All jewelry and gemstones are sourced from certified manufacturers and dealers who provide proper authentication and quality certificates. We ensure compliance with international standards including GIA, GJEPC, and other relevant certifications for precious metals and gemstones.
